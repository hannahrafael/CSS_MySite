A Few of my Favorite Projects:

Iterative Design Project – ND Picture Frame
Frame Project Final Memo (PDF)

During the Fall of 2018, I worked on a team of three members to create a 3D printed picture frame for the University bookstore. The first iteration of this frame, believe it or not, was rated one of the lowest in my Intro to Engineering Systems class. By considering the feedback my team receive from our peers, instructor, and bookstore employees, we redesigned the frame and completed a second iteration.

I am most proud that our frame ultimately won “Best Overall Design” in our class, and is currently displayed in Stinson-Remick Hall of Engineering at school.
